Challenge

  • 60+ Applications
  • 1000s of pages
  • 100s of videos
  • 150+ developers

Today it is April 1, deadline for release is December 8

How do you power up 150+ developers, 150+ testers and 100s of people in other roles in 30 days?

150 developers, 90% of these employees have never heard of WCAG, 90% of them have no idea of what accessibility is, no budget for training.

The first thing they did was bring in the experts.

Scope and Progress

Second order of business was to understand what applications were in scope and have a way to track their progress.

  • Assessed each application
  • Created tracking database
  • Created document repository
  • Created processes for tracking and escalating (using issue tracking system)

Leverage

  • Accessibility Experts
  • Common UI Components

  • Support and Steer
  • Tools
